# Public Skill Triage

Prefer a central `ai-skills` backlog entry when most of the following are true:

- the idea solves a need that is likely to recur across repositories or teams
- the workflow is tool-agnostic or can be expressed in tool-agnostic terms
- the skill would improve daily AI-agent work beyond one private codebase
- the idea is not only an internal policy, credential, or company-specific
  process

Prefer local-only capture when the idea is tightly coupled to one repository,
one company-internal system, or one private policy set.
